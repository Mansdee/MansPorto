About me

I'm a digital creator focusing on visual design and user experience development. With a background as a freelancer in UI/UX design, video editing, and other design fields, I have handled various creative projects that demand high creativity, rigor, and a deep understanding of audience needs.

I believe that design is not just about visual appearance, but also about how a piece of work can effectively convey a message and provide a pleasant experience for users. In every project, I always try to combine strong aesthetics with a functional technical approach.
